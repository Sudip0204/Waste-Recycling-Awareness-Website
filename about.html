Know Your Waste

Learn how to segregate common household waste materials.

Plastic

Rinse containers and check local guidelines. Typically includes bottles, jugs, and tubs.

Organic

Includes fruit and vegetable scraps, coffee grounds, and yard trimmings. Ideal for composting.

Metal

Recycle aluminum and steel cans. Ensure they are clean and empty before disposal.

Paper & Cardboard

Recycle newspapers, magazines, and flattened cardboard boxes. Keep it dry and clean.
